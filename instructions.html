INSTRUCTIONS

Before the experiment begins we will have a quick check to make sure your audio works well enough for the experiment.

You will listen to streams of words. The streams of words don't always make a lot of sense. Some streams will make more sense than others. Please try to listen to all of the words and look at the cross in the centre of the screen at all times.

At the end of each stream of words you will be asked whether you've heard a four-word phrase. You will not hear these exact phrases during the experiment but an example of a four-word phrase could be:

ask him this thing

from my old car

sit in that tree

If you think you heard a four word phrase please press 'y' at the end of the word stream and only when prompted. If you didn't hear any four word phrases please press 'n' at the end of the word stream and only when prompted.

At the end of the experiment you will be asked some additional questions to assess how well you have been able to pay attention.
